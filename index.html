Mission

A WARM AND WELCOMING HOME FOR EVERYTHING JEWISH, A PLACE WHERE EVERY INDIVIDUAL AND FAMILY CAN CONNECT, BELONG, AND GROW. GUIDED BY THE VISION AND DIRECTION OF THE LUBAVITCHER REBBE OBM, WE ARE DEDICATED TO ENRICHING AND INSPIRING JEWISH LIFE WITH JOY, MEANING, AND PURPOSE. THROUGH OUR WEEKLY SHABBAT SERVICES, CAMP GAN ISRAEL, HOLIDAY CELEBRATIONS, AND DYNAMIC PROGRAMMING FOR CHILDREN, TEENS, YOUNG ADULTS, WOMEN, MEN, AND SENIORS, WE FOSTER A VIBRANT COMMUNITY WHERE JUDAISM IS ALIVE, ACCESSIBLE, AND DEEPLY PERSONAL FOR ALL.
